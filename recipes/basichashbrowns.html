Basic Hash Browns

How to make:

This recipe makes three delicious diner-style hash browns that are crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside. These are sure to make your mouth water! Add a touch of ketchup to your hash browns and enjoy an American favorite!

Below we will go in depth on what exactly you need to create your delicious hashbrowns. We hope that you enjoy these as much as we did! Let us know in the comments below How much you like them!

Ingredients

  • 2 peeled russet potatoes
  • 3 tablespoons of clarified butter
  • Salt and Black pepper
  • 1 pinch of cayenne pepper
  • 1 pinch of paprika
